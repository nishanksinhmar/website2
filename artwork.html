  • MR.TREE

    • Year of Creation: 2022
    • Exhibited At: India Art Fair, New Delhi
    • Deep Meaning: Symbolizes pain and struggle.
    • Impact on Society: Highlights resilience and hope.

    "Mr. Tree" is a poignant mixed media piece by **Nishank**, depicting a solitary tree symbolizing pain and struggle. The artwork masterfully blends dark hues and textures to evoke a sense of enduring resilience amidst adversity.

  • MR. BALLOON

    • Year of Creation: 2023
    • Exhibited At: Jehangir Art Gallery, Mumbai
    • Deep Meaning: Freedom and imagination.
    • Impact on Society: Encourages limitless possibilities.

    I Can Fly
    "Blue Balloon" is a whimsical creation by **Nishank**, featuring a blue balloon with 33 teeth that can magically fly. Set against the vibrant backdrop of India, this artwork emphasizes the balloon's incredible ability to soar through the skies, symbolizing freedom and the limitless possibilities of imagination.

  • MR. BUTTERFLYYYY

    • Year of Creation: 2021
    • Exhibited At: National Gallery of Modern Art, Bangalore
    • Deep Meaning: Harmony between art and sound.
    • Impact on Society: Fosters creativity and joy.

    I Can also fly
    MR. "Butterfly" is a delicate artwork crafted entirely from pencil colors, showcasing a vibrant butterfly that loves to listen to music. The piece highlights the butterfly's whimsical nature and its ethereal presence, symbolizing the harmony between art and sound.
