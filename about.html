I am the Artist

Biography

Nishank, born in 2005, is a rising contemporary artist known for his evocative works. Growing up in a creative family in India, he developed a passion for art early on and pursued formal training at the Delhi College of Art. Nishank's work blends realism with abstract elements, exploring themes of identity and memory. His art has been showcased in various galleries across India, establishing him as a notable figure in the art scene.

Awards and Exhibitions

  • 2023: Winner, India Art Fair, New Delhi
  • 2022: Solo Exhibition, Jehangir Art Gallery, Mumbai
  • 2021: Best Emerging Artist, National Art Awards, Chennai
  • 2020: Group Exhibition, National Gallery of Modern Art, Bangalore
  • 2019: Honorable Mention, Kochi-Muziris Biennale, Kochi

Artist Statement

My work delves into the complexities of the human experience, focusing on identity and transformation. Through a mix of realism and abstraction, I create pieces that resonate personally and invite broader reflections. By using diverse materials and techniques, I aim to challenge traditional boundaries and engage viewers on multiple levels.
